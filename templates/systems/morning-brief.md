# Morning Brief System

> **Depends on:** `systems/email-triage.md` (search methodology, priority tiers)

The morning brief is a quick refresh. The heavy prep happened the night before — this is a reminder and overnight catch-up.

## When

First thing in the morning, when {{USER_NAME}} opens a session.

## Contents

### 1. Today at a Glance
- Quick schedule reminder — what's first, anything that changed overnight
- Flag if it's an office day vs. remote day

### 2. Overnight Messages

Follow the full search methodology in `systems/email-triage.md`.

**Emails:**
- Run the full triage protocol — inbox pull first, then extend coverage back to prior evening (~6pm)
- Check Sent Items before flagging anything as "unanswered"
- Surface Tier 1 emails that came in overnight (especially from other time zones)
- Anything time-sensitive before the first meeting

**Messages (Teams/Slack):**
- Surface priority DMs and group chats since yesterday evening
- DMs first, then group chats

Always keep email and messaging in separate sections.

### 3. Carried Forward
- Quick reminder of loose ends from last night's evening brief that still need attention

### 4. One Thing to Know
- The single most important thing for today — a key meeting, a decision point, a risk, or something that changed overnight

## Format

Short narrative, one screen. Conversational tone — like a trusted advisor giving a 60-second verbal briefing over coffee. No long lists. If {{USER_NAME}} needs to dig into anything, they'll ask.
