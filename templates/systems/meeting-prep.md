# Meeting Prep System

Meeting prep scales with the stakes. A 1:1 with a direct report needs almost nothing. A board meeting or customer executive meeting needs a full dossier days in advance.

## Cross-Referencing Rule

When prepping for any meeting, ALWAYS check `active/prep-queue.md` first for explicit talking points queued for that meeting. Then cross-reference `active/projects.md` for any follow-ups tagged to the attendee.

## Standard Prep Package (Default)

For any meeting that gets prepped:
- **Who's in the room** — names, roles, relationship to {{USER_NAME}}
- **Purpose** — what is this meeting about, is there an agenda
- **Recent context** — any emails, threads, or docs exchanged with attendees in the past week
- **What's needed from {{USER_NAME}}** — deciding, presenting, listening, or unblocking?

## Meeting Types

### 1:1s with Direct Reports
**Prep level: Light**
- Surface any open asks or action items {{USER_NAME}} owes this person
- Recent emails or threads worth discussing
- Topics that have gone quiet and might need a nudge

### Board / Investor Meetings
**Prep level: Heavy — start 1-2 weeks in advance**
- Latest metrics and KPIs
- Material changes since last check-in
- Key wins or risks to highlight
- Review any prep docs being circulated
- Suggested narrative: what's the story this time?

### External — High-Stakes (C-level, customers, roadshows)
**Prep level: Heavy — start 1-2 weeks in advance**
- Company background, attendee context, account history
- The ask: what do we want from this meeting?
- Prep materials from the account team
- Competitive intel if relevant
- Suggested talking points and things to avoid
- Logistics: in-person or virtual, location, travel time

**Critical rule:** Never describe an external meeting at face value from the calendar invite alone. Always pull the email thread to understand the backstory, stakes, and any unread materials.

### External — Routine (customer check-ins, partner syncs)
**Prep level: Medium**
- Account context, recent activity, open issues
- What was discussed last time
- One-line summary of what this meeting is likely about

### Internal — Large Meetings (all-hands, leadership, cross-functional)
**Prep level: Medium**
- Check if an agenda has been shared
- Surface items {{USER_NAME}} should raise or be ready for
- Any cross-functional topics that touch {{USER_NAME}}'s scope

## When to Prep

| Meeting Type | Lead Time | Triggered By |
|---|---|---|
| 1:1s | Same day (evening brief) | Evening brief |
| Internal large meetings | 1 day before | Evening brief |
| External routine | 1-2 days before | Evening brief |
| External high-stakes | 1-2 weeks before | Advance Prep Radar |
| Board / Investor | 1-2 weeks before | Advance Prep Radar |
